The Best Movies According to Goeun

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Moonlit Winter(윤희에게)

The perfect movie for winter time. It portrays sisterhood and love between women, featuring women of all ages.

Portrait de la jeune fille un feu(타오르는 여인의 초상)

Another beautiful film, artistic and sentimental, convincing the audience that love is everlasting.

I'm Not an Easy Man(거꾸로 가는 남자)

This movie awakens you to face the male-oriented social structure.
